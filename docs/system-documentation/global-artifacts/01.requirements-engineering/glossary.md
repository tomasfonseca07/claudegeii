# Glossary

**Terms, Expressions and Acronyms (TEA) must be organized alphabetically.**

| **_TEA_** (EN) | **_TEA_** (PT) | **_Description_** (EN) |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **AD** | **DI** | Acronym for _Declaration of Interests_. |
| **Address** | **Morada** | Physical address of an asset or person. For the purposes of this system, address granularity is limited to municipality level (até concelho). |
| **Admin** | **Administrador** | System administrator responsible for approving or rejecting user registrations. Must provide a reason when rejecting a request. |
| **Attachment** | **Anexo** | File(s) that can be added to a declaration at submission time. |
| **Citizen** | **Cidadão** | A member of the public who can access the transparency portal to scrutinise political agents. Can submit anonymous complaints while authenticated. |
| **Citizen Card** | **Cartão de Cidadão** | Official Portuguese identification document used to verify the identity of journalists and citizens during registration. |
| **Complaint** | **Queixa** | A report submitted by any authenticated citizen regarding a political agent. Complaints are anonymous and can be submitted for any reason. |
| **CTZ** | **CID** | Acronym for _Citizen_. |
| **Declaration of Interests** | **Declaração de Interesses** | Main registration document submitted by a political agent. Describes all assets (real estate, salaries, participations, etc.) and their origin. Used to identify irregularities and conflicts of interest. Once submitted, it cannot be edited — a new declaration must be submitted instead. |
| **Declaration Status** | **Estado da Declaração** | The current state of a declaration (e.g., PENDING, VALIDATED, REJECTED). Only VALIDATED declarations are considered in consultations and analyses. |
| **EC** | **CE** | Acronym for _Ethics Committee_. |
| **Ethics Committee** | **Comissão de Ética** | Oversight body with full access to the system. Receives and analyses declarations of interests, evaluates complaints, and can request new declarations from political agents. |
| **Function** | **Função** | The hierarchical position of a political agent within an organisation (e.g., CEO, Director). This refers to seniority/hierarchy, not whether the role is public or private. |
| **Illicit Enrichment** | **Enriquecimento Ilícito** | Wealth accumulated by a political agent that cannot be justified by their official salary or declared income. |
| **Institution** | **Instituição** | An organisation (company, political party, foundation, institute, or association) associated with a political agent's positions or participations. |
| **INST** | **INST** | Acronym for _Institution_. |
| **JRN** | **JRN** | Acronym for _Journalist_. |
| **Journalist** | **Jornalista** | Registered user who can analyse declarations and income evolution of political agents. Must be verified via a valid citizen card. Member of the Journalists' Union. |
| **Organisation Type** | **Tipo de Organização** | Category of organisation associated with a side income or asset (e.g., civil construction, healthcare). Does not refer to political party affiliation. |
| **PA** | **AP** | Acronym for _Political Agent_. |
| **Political Agent** | **Agente Político** | A person performing a political function (e.g., deputy, minister, councillor, mayor) who is required to submit declarations of interests to the system. |
| **SA** | **AS** | Acronym for _System Administrator_. |
| **Sensitive Data** | **Dados Sensíveis** | Information that must be partially omitted depending on the user's role. Includes monetary values and addresses. |
| **Side Income** | **Rendimento Lateral** | Income of a political agent that falls outside the scope of their political role (e.g., rental income, private employment). |
| **TP** | **PT** | Acronym for _Transparency Portal_. |
| **Transparency Portal** | **Portal da Transparência** | The IT platform that supports the registration, evaluation, and analysis of declarations of interests by political agents. |
| **VD** | **DV** | Acronym for _Validated Declaration_. |
| **Validated Declaration** | **Declaração Validada** | A declaration of interests that has been reviewed and approved by the Ethics Committee. Only validated declarations are included in consultations and analyses. |
